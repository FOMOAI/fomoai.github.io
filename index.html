Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 25th May 2026

FOMO AI ("we," "our," or "us") operates the FOMO AI mobile application (the "App"). We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how data is handled.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how you can manage your privacy choices through our integrated consent tools.

1. Information Collected Automatically via Google AdMob

Our App does not require user registration or accounts, and we do not collect direct personal details like your name, phone number, or email address.

However, to fund our App through advertisements, we utilize the Google AdMob SDK. Google AdMob automatically collects and processes specific data types to serve, measure, and secure advertisements. This includes:

  • Device Identifiers: Your device's unique IP address and mobile advertising identifiers (such as Apple's IDFA on iOS or Google's Advertising ID on Android).
  • Approximate Location: General geographic data derived from your IP address, used to serve regionally relevant ads.
  • App Interactions: Data regarding your interactions with advertisements, such as ad clicks, views, and timestamps.
  • Diagnostics: Technical data including crash logs, system performance metrics, and app load times.

2. How Your Information is Used

The data collected by Google AdMob is utilized for the following core purposes:

  • Ad Delivery & Optimization: Showing you relevant advertisements.
  • Analytics: Measuring the performance and viewability of ad campaigns.
  • Fraud Prevention & Security: Detecting fraudulent ad clicks, blocking bot traffic, and ensuring app stability.

3. Google UMP Consent Mechanism & User Choice

We implement the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK to comply with global privacy regulations, including the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, and relevant US State Privacy Laws (such as CCPA/CPRA).

  • Explicit Choice: Depending on your geographic region, when you open the App, the Google UMP consent banner will prompt you to explicitly grant or deny permission for data collection and personalized tracking.
  • Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads:
    • If you grant consent, Google and its certified ad partners will serve personalized ads tailored to your interests.
    • If you refuse consent, Google AdMob will serve non-personalized (contextual) ads. These ads do not track your long-term behavior but still require basic device identifiers and approximate location to function and prevent fraud.
  • Revoking or Changing Consent: You have the right to change your mind at any time. You can easily reload the Google UMP consent form by navigating to the [Settings / Privacy Options] menu inside our App and clicking on "Manage Consent" or "Privacy Preferences."
  • Device-Level Controls: You can opt-out of personalized tracking across all apps via your operating system's settings (e.g., "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).

4. Third-Party Links and Policies

Google AdMob is operated by Google LLC. We strongly encourage you to review their specific data practices by visiting the Google Advertising Privacy & Terms page.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not structured to attract children under the age of 13 (or 16 within the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal identifiers from children. If we discover a child's data has been gathered, we will take immediate steps to remove it from our ad partner's systems.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our data practices or shifts in global legal requirements. We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page whenever a change is made.
